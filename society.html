Our Societies

The IEEE Student Branch at St. Thomas College of Engineering and Technology hosts several societies that allow students to focus on specialized fields, collaborate with peers, and enhance technical expertise.

Active Societies

  • IEEE Computer Society: Computing, software development, and emerging technologies.
  • IEEE Robotics & Automation Society: Robotics projects, automation, and AI integration.
  • IEEE Power & Energy Society: Energy systems, renewable technologies, sustainable engineering.
  • IEEE Communications Society: Networking, wireless communication, IoT innovation.
  • IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE): Encouraging and supporting women in technical education and careers.

  • Research & Project Development
  • Competitions & Hackathons
