About Student Branch

Empowering Students with Technology & Leadership

SB Overview

The IEEE Student Branch at St. Thomas College of Engineering and Technology, Kannur, established in December 2023, is a vibrant student-led community driven by innovation, learning, and impact.

Active Members: 30+   |   Events Organised: 17

Objectives

  • Empower students through workshops, tech talks, hackathons, and competitions.
  • Encourage curiosity, creativity, and collaboration in technology projects.
  • Develop technical expertise, leadership skills, and a strong professional network.
  • Nurture future-ready engineers who innovate responsibly and contribute meaningfully to society.

Activities

  • Workshops & Seminars
  • Tech Talks by Industry Experts
  • Hackathons & Competitions
  • Collaborative Projects & Real-World Problem Solving
